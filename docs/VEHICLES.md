Across fleet/aggregator platforms like Uber, Ola, and others (Rapido, Porter, BluSmart, etc.), “vehicle types” are essentially **service categories mapped to vehicle classes + use-case (passenger, goods, premium, etc.)**.

Below is a **complete, system-level classification** used in India 👇

---

# 🚗 1. Passenger Ride Categories (Cars)

## Economy / Budget

- Hatchbacks (WagonR, Alto, Kwid)
- Categories:
  - UberGo / Ola Mini

- Use case: Low-cost daily commute

## Sedan / Standard

- Dzire, Etios, Xcent
- Categories:
  - Uber Sedan / Ola Sedan

- More comfort than hatchbacks

## Premium

- Honda City, Hyundai Verna, Ciaz
- Categories:
  - Uber Premier / Ola Prime Sedan

## SUV / MUV

- Ertiga, Innova, Marazzo
- Categories:
  - UberXL / Ola Prime SUV

- 6–7 passengers

## Luxury

- BMW, Mercedes, Audi
- Categories:
  - Uber Black / Uber Lux (limited in India)

---

# 🛵 2. Two-Wheeler / Bike Taxi

- Motorcycles / Scooters (100–200cc)
- Platforms:
  - Uber Moto, Ola Bike, Rapido

### Use Case

- Short distances, heavy traffic areas

---

# 🚐 3. Auto Rickshaws (3-Wheelers)

- Standard CNG Autos
- E-rickshaws (in some cities)

### Categories

- Uber Auto
- Ola Auto

---

# 🚕 4. Electric Vehicles (EV Categories)

- Electric hatchbacks (Tigor EV, Xpres-T)
- Electric autos
- Electric bikes

### Platforms

- BluSmart (all-electric fleet)
- Uber Green / Ola EV options (city-dependent)

---

# 🚚 5. Goods / Logistics Vehicles

Used in platforms like Porter, Uber Freight, LoadShare.

## Light Commercial Vehicles (LCV)

- Tata Ace (Chhota Hathi)
- Pickup trucks

## Medium Trucks

- Tata 407
- Bolero Pickup

## Heavy Trucks

- 14 ft, 17 ft, 20 ft trucks
- Container trucks

## 3-Wheeler Goods

- Cargo autos

---

# 🚐 6. Rentals & Outstation Vehicles

Same physical vehicles, but different **service mode**:

- Hourly rentals (with driver)
- Outstation trips (intercity)
- Multi-day bookings

Vehicle types:

- Hatchback
- Sedan
- SUV

---

# 🚘 7. Self-Drive Cars (Different Segment)

Platforms like Zoomcar (not driver-based fleets):

- Hatchbacks
- Sedans
- SUVs
- Luxury cars (limited)

---

# 🚌 8. High-Capacity / Shared Mobility

- Vans (Tempo Traveller – 9 to 17 seater)
- Shuttle buses (office commute)

---

# 🚛 9. Specialized Fleet Types

## Delivery Vehicles

- Bikes (food delivery: Zomato, Swiggy)
- Scooters (quick commerce)

## Ambulance / Emergency (rare integration)

- Partnered emergency fleets in some apps

## Wheelchair Accessible Vehicles (WAV)

- Modified vans (limited availability)

---

# 🧭 Platform Mapping (Simplified)

| Category  | Example Platforms         |
| --------- | ------------------------- |
| Cars      | Uber, Ola                 |
| Bikes     | Rapido, Uber Moto         |
| Autos     | Uber Auto, Ola Auto       |
| EV Fleets | BluSmart                  |
| Goods     | Porter, Uber Freight      |
| Rentals   | Uber Rentals, Ola Rentals |

---

# ⚠️ Key Technical Constraints

- Vehicle age limits (typically < 8 years for cars)
- Commercial registration required
- Category eligibility depends on:
  - City
  - Demand
  - Platform policy

---

# ✅ Bottom Line

All fleet services broadly fall into:

1. Passenger (cars, bikes, autos)
2. Electric variants
3. Goods/logistics vehicles
4. Rentals/outstation
5. Shared & specialized transport
