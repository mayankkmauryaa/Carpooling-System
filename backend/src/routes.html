🔄 Sagas NEW

Event-driven saga orchestrator for transactions

Booking Saga - Reserve seat, process payment, confirm booking

Cancellation Saga - Cancel booking, refund payment, release seats

Payout Saga - Validate payout, calculate split, process transfer

Sagas are triggered internally via event consumers (Kafka/in-memory)

Note: Most endpoints require authentication. Add header: Authorization: Bearer <token>
